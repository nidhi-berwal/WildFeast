Birds

Learn about different birds and their characteristics.

Parrot

Parrots are colorful birds known for their ability to mimic sounds.

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds of prey with excellent vision.

Peacock

Peacocks are famous for their stunning feather display.

Owl

Owls are nocturnal birds known for their wisdom.

Flamingo

Flamingos are pink birds that thrive in shallow lakes.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds adapted to cold climates.
